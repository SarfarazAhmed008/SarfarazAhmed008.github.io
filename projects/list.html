Frontliners (Networking and service based application for doctors)

Networking app for doctors that provides all the services to ease up the life of our frontline warriors, the doctors. It has several features like Case Discussion, Trivia (Medical Quiz), Safety Feature, Medical Jobs and News, Job Search, Workplace Reviews, Offline Drug Directory, Hospital Updates and much more. Currently, more than 7.5k++ doctors of Bangladesh are registered in our services and actively using it.

Technology used: Flutter, Provider, REST APIs, Firebase, Local Persistent Storage, Push Notifications etc.
